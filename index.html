Frederic Zhang | 张真

I'm currently a PhD student at the Australian National University, working with Prof. Stephen Gould. Prior to this, as part of a partnership program, I completed a bachelor degree of science in automation from Beijing Institute of Technology and a bachelor degree of engineering in mechatronics (research and development) with first-class honours from the Australian National University, where I worked with Prof. Yuchao Dai and Prof. Richard Hartley.

My Primary research interest is in localising and understanding human-object interactions. But I'm generally interested in most computer vision and machine learning topics.
